Benjamin Franklin's 13 Virtues

Inspired by Benjamin Franklin's systematic approach to self-improvement. At age 20, Franklin identified 13 virtues essential to his character and tracked his progress daily. He focused on one virtue each week, creating a perpetual cycle of growth. Identity Builder brings this timeless practice into the modern age, helping you build the character you aspire to through deliberate daily practice.

1. Temperance

Eat not to dullness; drink not to elevation.

2. Silence

Speak not but what may benefit others or yourself.

3. Order

Let all your things have their places; let each part of your business have its time.

4. Resolution

Resolve to perform what you ought; perform without fail what you resolve.

5. Frugality

Make no expense but to do good to others or yourself.

6. Industry

Lose no time; be always employed in something useful.

7. Sincerity

Use no hurtful deceit; think innocently and justly.

8. Justice

Wrong none by doing injuries, or omitting the benefits that are your duty.

9. Moderation

Avoid extremes; forbear resenting injuries so much as you think they deserve.

10. Cleanliness

Tolerate no uncleanliness in body, clothes, or habitation.

11. Tranquility

Be not disturbed at trifles, or at accidents common or unavoidable.

12. Chastity

Rarely use venery but for health or offspring.

13. Humility

Imitate Jesus and Socrates.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Identity Builder is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your personal growth journey should remain personal.

Data Collection

Identity Builder collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Habit names and descriptions
  • Identity labels you assign to habits
  • Habit completion records and dates
  • Experiments you've tried with each habit
  • Weekly retrospective notes
  • Custom scheduling preferences

Local Storage Only

All of your data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not:

  • Transmit your data to any servers
  • Collect analytics or usage statistics
  • Track your behavior or habits
  • Sell or share your information with third parties
  • Require account creation or sign-in

Data Export and Backup

You have complete control over your data. Identity Builder includes CSV export functionality, allowing you to:

  • Export all your habit data at any time
  • Create backups of your progress
  • Import data back into the app
  • Move your data to other platforms if desired

App Permissions

Identity Builder may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To remind you about your habits (optional, you control this)
  • Files Access: Only when you choose to export or import CSV backups

Third-Party Services

Identity Builder does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Identity Builder does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we don't collect any personal information, the app can be safely used by users of all ages.

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date and notify users through the app or this website.

Open Source

Identity Builder is open source. You can review the complete source code to verify our privacy practices at our GitHub repository.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the App Store.
